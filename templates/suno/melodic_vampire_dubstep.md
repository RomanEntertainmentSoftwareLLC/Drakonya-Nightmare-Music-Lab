# Melodic Vampire Dubstep Template

## Core Sound

Dark melodic dubstep with gothic nightclub atmosphere, cinematic bass drops, haunting minor-key melodies, cathedral-scale synths, and dramatic emotional tension.

## Prompt Ingredients

- melodic dubstep
- gothic electro
- vampire nightclub atmosphere
- heavy cinematic bass drop
- haunting vocal chops
- minor-key chord progression
- cathedral reverb
- dark romantic energy
- red neon / blood moon mood
- polished EDM mix
- 140 BPM feel

## Example Suno Style Prompt

Dark melodic dubstep with a vampire nightclub atmosphere, haunting female vocal chops, emotional minor-key chords, cathedral synths, cinematic bass drops, dramatic orchestral stabs, red neon gothic club energy, polished EDM production, intense but beautiful, 140 BPM.

## Avoid

- naming specific artists
- copied lyrics
- parodying real songs
- overly generic EDM wording
