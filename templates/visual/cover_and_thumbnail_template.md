## Visual QA Checklist

- Matches selected release lane
- Matches target audience/use case
- Does not force gothic/dark aesthetics unless selected
- Looks usable at small thumbnail size
- Avoids copyrighted logos and characters
- Avoids celebrity likenesses
- Avoids fake artist impersonation
- Does not use a new logo when an approved logo already exists
- Has optional logo-on and logo-off variants when useful
