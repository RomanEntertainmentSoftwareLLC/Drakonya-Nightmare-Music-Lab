# Drakonya Nightmare AI Music Lab

Drakonya Nightmare AI Music Lab is a standalone Hermes-powered AI music and media production system.

Its purpose is to generate, package, publish, and analyze AI-assisted music releases for Drakonya Nightmare Studios.

## Core Workflow

1. Research genres and niches.
2. Generate music concepts and Suno prompts.
3. Generate or collect tracks.
4. Score tracks for quality.
5. Create covers, thumbnails, and video-loop prompts.
6. Assemble crossfaded compilations.
7. Combine audio and video.
8. Prepare DistroKid and YouTube metadata.
9. Track views, streams, revenue, costs, and ROI.
10. Feed analytics into the next production batch.

## Design Rule

This project is standalone.

It must not depend on ACC's hardcoded path:

    /opt/openclaw/.openclaw/workspace

The project root is controlled by:

    DRAKONYA_ROOT=/opt/drakonya-nightmare-music-lab
